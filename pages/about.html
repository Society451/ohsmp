About OHSMP

The OHSMP is a Society dedicated to maintaining a private Minecraft server for all OHS students to join and use free of charge. We will be running the server in Java, with the specific version to be decided at a later date once sufficient funding has been provided by Student Life.

For more information, visit our Official Society Canvas page.
