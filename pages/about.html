About OHSMP

The Official Minecraft Server of Stanford Online High School

OHSMP is a student-run Minecraft server for the Stanford Online High School community. The server provides a creative and collaborative space for students to connect outside of class.

The server features custom rules designed to encourage faction creation, alliances, and friendly competition.

Join us in-game at play.ohsmp.com if you are a current OHS student!
